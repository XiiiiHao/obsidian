### 傅里叶级数
$\displaystyle f(t) = \frac{a_0}{2} + \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \left[ a_n \cos\frac{n \pi }{l}x + b_n \sin \frac{n\pi}{l}x \right]$
$\displaystyle a_{n} =  \frac{1}{l}\int^{l}_{-l}f(x) \sin \frac{n\pi}{l}xdx$
$\displaystyle b_{n} =  \frac{1}{l}\int^{l}_{-l}f(x) \cos \frac{n\pi}{l}xdx$

> [!solution]+ 幂级数求和的方法

1.整式型
	$\displaystyle \sum^{\infty}_{n=0}x^{n}= \frac{1}{1-x} ,-1 <x<1$
	$\displaystyle \sum^{\infty}_{n=0}(n+1)x^{n}= \frac{1}{(1-x) ^{2}} ,-1 <x<1$
	$\displaystyle \sum^{\infty}_{n=0}(n+2)(n+1)x^{n}= \frac{2}{(1-x) ^{3}} ,-1 <x<1$
2.分式型
	$\displaystyle \sum^{\infty}_{n=0}\frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} = - ln(1-x), \quad -1 \leq x < 1$
	$\displaystyle \sum^{\infty}_{n=0}\frac{x^{2n+1}}{2n+1} = \frac{1}{2}ln \frac{1+x}{1-x}, \quad -1<x< 1$
	$\displaystyle \sum^{\infty}_{n=0}\frac{(-1)^{n}x^{2n+1}}{2n+1} = \arctan x, \quad -1\leq x< 1$
