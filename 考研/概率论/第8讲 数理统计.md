
### $\displaystyle \chi^{2}分布$
* 概念：相互独立，都服从标准正态分布，$\displaystyle X= \sum^{n}_{i=1}X_{i}^{2} ～ \chi^{2}(n)$
* $\displaystyle E(\frac{1}{2})\sim \chi^{2}(2)$
* $\displaystyle EX=n,DX=2n$

### $\displaystyle t分布$
* 概念：$\displaystyle X～N(0,1),Y～ \chi^{2}(n)，相互独立，t= \frac{X}{\sqrt{ Y/n }}～t(n)$
* $\displaystyle t_{1-\alpha}(n)=-t_{\alpha}(n)$

### $\displaystyle F分布$
* 概念：
* $\displaystyle F～F( n_{1},n_{2}),则 \frac{1}{F}～F(n_{2},n_{1})$
* $\displaystyle F_{1-\alpha}(n_{1},n_{2})= \frac{1}{F_{\alpha}(n_{2},n_{1})}$
* $\displaystyle F_{0.5}(n,n)=1$
* $\displaystyle 若t \sim t(n),则t^{2} \sim F(1,n)$



### ==正态总体==下的常用结论

* $\displaystyle \bar{X} \sim N\left( \mu, \frac{\delta^{2}}{n} \right); \quad 即 \frac{\sqrt{ n }(\bar{X}-\mu)}{\delta} \sim N(0,1)$
* $\displaystyle 若\delta 未知，上式变为\frac{\sqrt{ n }(\bar{X}-\mu)}{S} \sim t(n-1)，若t \sim t(n-1),t^{2} \sim F(1,n-1)$
* $\displaystyle \frac{1}{\delta^{2}} \sum^{n}_{i=1}(X_{i}-\mu)^{2} \sim \chi^{2}(n)$
* $\displaystyle 若\mu未知，上式变为\frac{(n-1)S^{2}}{\delta^{2}}= \sum^{n}_{i=1}\left( \frac{X_{i}- \bar{X}}{\delta} \right)^{2} \sim \chi^{2}(n-1)$
